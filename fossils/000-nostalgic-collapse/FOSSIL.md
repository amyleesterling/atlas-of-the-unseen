# Fossil 000 — Nostalgic Collapse

## Cause of death

Particles maintain a pairwise global memory ledger. Nearby encounters permanently increase the attraction between each pair. Nothing forgets. The total attractive coupling therefore rises monotonically, and almost every sufficiently connected run ends in one nostalgic clump.

## Why preserve it

- It demonstrates that forgetting is load-bearing.
- It makes an invisible global ledger visible as a counter.
- It proves that failed universes are artifacts, not trash.
- It gives the laboratory a first comparative specimen.
