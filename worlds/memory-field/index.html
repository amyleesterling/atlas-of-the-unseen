Laws Elsewhere · Experiment 001

What if memory were a force?

This is a tiny invented universe. Glowing particles move through a field, leaving colored traces and changing what they carry inside. Your job is to discover the rule by watching and interfering.

1 · WatchTap Begin and let the world run for 10–20 seconds. You should see obvious movement.
2 · SelectUse Observe / select, then tap a particle. A white path will grow behind it.
3 · EraseChoose Erase memory. Swipe empty space, then swipe directly over a particle. Compare the effects.

Your question: Is attraction stored in the particle, left behind in space, or created by resemblance between colors?

Nothing is sent to an API. Your hypothesis stays in this browser and can be exported as JSON.
